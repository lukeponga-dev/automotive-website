About Us

Welcome to Our Automotive Garage, located at the heart of Hamilton on 137 Bankwood Road. With over 25 years of dedicated service to our community, we have become a cornerstone for vehicle care, offering comprehensive automotive solutions for all makes and models.

Our vision is to be the leading Automotive Workshop on the North Shore, offering a refreshing and professional approach to mechanical servicing. We are leaders in technology and staff training, striving to provide top levels of customer satisfaction and give back to our community and the automotive industry.

At Our Automotive Garage, we understand the importance of trust and transparency. That's why we offer upfront pricing, detailed explanations of all services, and a commitment to customer satisfaction. Our goal is to keep you informed and your vehicle safe on the road.

Whether you need a Warrant of Fitness, engine diagnostics, brake repairs, or air-conditioning services, you can count on us for reliable, professional, and affordable service. We also offer convenient amenities such as a free local shuttle service and courtesy cars to ensure your experience with us is as smooth as your drive.

Driven by passion and precision, Our Automotive Garage is your trusted partner for all your automotive needs. Visit us today and experience the difference quality service makes.
