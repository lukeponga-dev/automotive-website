Our Services

Servicing

Vehicle servicing is an important requirement to keep your vehicle reliable and safe.

  • Engine Oil & Filter: Change engine oil & filter, visual check
  • Engine Oil & Filter + Safety inspection: Inspect air filter, cabin filter, brakes, suspension, steering, exhaust, lights, drivetrain, and cooling system.

Repairs

Fix those faults before they become major. Let us know what car repairs you require, and we can give you a quote.

  • Brake Pads & Rotors: Install new brake pads and rotors for cars and SUVs.
  • Diagnostic Scan: Identify faults and provide repairs.
  • CV Boot Replacement: Protect CV joints by replacing damaged boots.
